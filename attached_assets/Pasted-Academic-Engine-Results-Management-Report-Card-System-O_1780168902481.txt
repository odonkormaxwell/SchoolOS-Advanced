Academic Engine, Results Management & Report Card System
Objective

Build the complete Academic Engine for Edulex.

This module will become the core academic system used by schools to:

Manage academic years
Manage classes and streams
Manage subjects
Configure assessments
Record scores
Generate results
Approve results
Publish results
Generate report cards
Promote students
Generate academic reports

The final system should be suitable for KG, Primary, and JHS schools in Ghana.

Role permissions previously defined must be enforced throughout this module.

MODULE 15: ROLE ENFORCEMENT

School Owner

View reports only.

Cannot enter scores.

Cannot publish results.

Headteacher

Approve and publish results.

Manage academic structure.

Teacher

Enter scores only for assigned classes and subjects.

Cannot approve or publish.

Parent

View only linked child records.

Student

View only own records.
