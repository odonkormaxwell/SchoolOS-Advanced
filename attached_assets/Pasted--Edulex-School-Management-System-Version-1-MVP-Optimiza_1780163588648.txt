# Edulex School Management System - Version 1 MVP Optimization & Stabilization

## Project Overview

You are working on Edulex, a modern School Management System SaaS built for Basic Schools, Primary Schools, and Junior High Schools in Ghana.

The current system functions more like a prototype than a production-ready SaaS. Several modules appear unfinished, buttons are not functioning correctly, reports are messy, settings are not wired properly, and the overall user experience feels overly complex.

Your goal is to transform the system into a clean, premium, modern MVP suitable for onboarding real schools.

---
