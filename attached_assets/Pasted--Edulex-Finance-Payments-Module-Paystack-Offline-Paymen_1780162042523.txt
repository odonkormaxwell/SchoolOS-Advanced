# Edulex Finance & Payments Module – Paystack + Offline Payments

## Objective

Build a complete Finance & Payments Module for Edulex.

Edulex is a School Management SaaS for Basic Schools, Primary Schools, and Junior High Schools in Ghana.

The finance module should support:

1. Online payments through Paystack
2. Offline/manual payment recording
3. Parent fee payments
4. Student invoices
5. Automatic receipts
6. Partial payments
7. Accountant reconciliation
8. Edulex subscription billing
9. Multi-tenant school payment settings

The final system should feel like a polished commercial SaaS product, not a prototype.

---

# 1. PAYMENT ARCHITECTURE

Edulex is a multi-tenant SaaS.

Each school should be able to connect its own Paystack account.

Do not use one Paystack account for all schools.

## School Fee Collection Flow

Parent pays School.

Payment should go directly into the school’s Paystack account.

Example:

Parent → School Paystack Account

## Edulex Subscription Flow

School pays Edulex.

Payment should go into Edulex’s Paystack account.

Example:

School → Edulex Paystack Account

These two payment flows must be kept separate.

# 8. PAY NOW WORKFLOW

When Pay Now is clicked:

1. Generate unique invoice payment reference.
2. Initialize Paystack transaction.
3. Redirect or open Paystack checkout.
4. Parent completes payment.
5. Redirect back to Edulex.
6. Verify transaction from backend.
7. Process Paystack webhook.
8. Mark invoice as paid or partially paid.
9. Update student balance.
10. Generate receipt.
11. Notify parent and accountant.

Never mark payment as successful without Paystack verification.
